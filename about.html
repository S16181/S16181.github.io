AFP Units

Protective Services Officer (PSO)

Protective Service Officers are frontline defenders who protect key locations and people at high-security sites such as the River City Bank, the CourtHouse, and Jewellery Store, and other high risk areas. They’re trained to deter, detect and respond to criminal and national security threats, operating as an armed protective presence.

Image Placeholder

Tactical Response Team (TRT)

The Tactical Response Team is an elite capability that responds to serious, high-risk and fast-moving incidents in challenging environments—across Australia and offshore. Operators are on standby to deploy domestically and internationally in support of major investigations and emergency responses. The team uses specialist tactics, rapidly deployable technology and advanced capabilities to resolve and disrupt serious threats to the community. It’s the AFP’s highest end of tactical response when the stakes are at their peak.
